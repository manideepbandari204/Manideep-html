About College

Vaageswari college of engineering is one of the leading institutions dedicated to providing quality education and developing skilled professionals. The college offers modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and excellent infrastructure. Students are encouraged to participate in technical events, cultural activities, and sports. The institution focuses on innovation, research, and overall personality development. Strong industry partnerships help students gain practical knowledge and excellent placement opportunities.
